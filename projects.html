3D Animation Blender Davinci Resolve

CMSA Donation Video (2023)

The Curtin Muslim Students Association got in contact with me to create this 3D animation video to help increase funds towards providing daily meals for Curtin Uni Staff and Students observing fasting in Ramadan.

All the animation was created in Blender and sound effects were added in Davinci with extra colour-grading and compositing

Short-film Wildlife-film Davinci Resolve After-Effects

Amboseli (2022)

Last year I was able to visit my home country of Kenya after 14 years. During my journey I was able to visit Amboseli National Park, which this film is about. Amboseli is a place where the land, water and hills all meet. A perfect place for the animals of Kenya.
